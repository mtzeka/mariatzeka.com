Maria is a Chicago-based visual artist and developer. In 2011 she obtained her B.F.A. in Interactive Arts and Media from Columbia College, Chicago, where she currently teaches courses in programming and web development part-time.

While Maria began as a new media artist her work has shifted to film as a medium; with a focus on photography and cinematography. Her portrais of musicians have been published by outlets such as Chicago Reader, Electronic Beats, Tiny Little Mixtapes, Fact, and Juno Plus.
