About

Maria has been using the same Nikon FG 35mm camera since her sophomore year of high school, when she first attended a black and white darkroom photography class. She continues to exclusively work with film and focuses on capturing the quiet moments found in everyday life. In recent years she has also been taking portraits of various Chicago electronic musicians where her photos have been used in publications such as the Chicago Reader, FACT, Juno Plus, XLR8R, and Electronic Beats.
